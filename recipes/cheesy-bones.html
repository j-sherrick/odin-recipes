Cheesy Bones

Delicious golden cheesy doggy treats

These delicious cheesy doggy treats are sure to be a hit!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups flour
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbsp of oil
  • 1 1/3 cups of water

Steps

  1. Combine flour and cheese
  2. Stir in oil and water
  3. Roll on floured cutting board to 3/8" thick
  4. Cut with bone shaped cutter
  5. Place on ungreased cookie sheet
  6. Bake in 250-350 degrees oven until dry
